Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Dummii sticker work?

You simply load your sticker with details you put on the app, then each person in your group taps their phone on the sticker to instantly recieve their payment link. The sticker contains a unique link that opens the Allospend app with your group's payment details.

What payment methods are supported?

Allospend works with Cash App, Venmo, and PayPal. More payment providers coming soon!

Is my data safe and private?

Absolutely. We use bank-grade encryption and never sell your data. Your financial information stays private and secure.

When will I get beta access?

Beta invitations are sent on a rolling basis. You'll receive an email within 1 week of signing up with your download link.

Can I use it for recurring expenses?

Yes! Our management tool is perfect for rent, utilities, and other regular shared expenses. You set it up once and automate your group payments.

What if someone doesn't pay?

Allospend includes gentle reminder systems and relationship health tracking to help you maintain positive group dynamics.
